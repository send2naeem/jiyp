Our Mission

The mission of Encompass Medical Centre is to promote the health and well-being of the local population by providing accessible, high quality medical care for people of all ages. Encompass Medical Centre is commited to providing services that will exceed the expectations of our patients.

The Facility

Encompass Medical Centre will be located at 533 Blackburn Road, Mt Waverley. The medical centre has ample onsite parking and is easily accessible from the Monash Freeway. The medical centre has 12 consulting rooms, a treatment room, a procedure room and a playroom. Pathology collection is available on site via Melbourne Pathology.

Free parking is available on site via Lionel Road. Please note that the car park exit is via Blackburn Road.

Latest Services

WorkHealth

Encompass Medical Centre GPs can perform a wide range of corporate health services for your staff which can be tailored to suit your business requirements. Please contact the clinic to discuss how we can assist you.

Aged Care

Many of the doctors at Encompass Medical Centre have a special interest in Aged Care. Our caring doctors understand the often complex requirements of elderly patients and are fully qualified and experienced in developing and implementing health management plans, chronic disease management and provide ongoing support.

Women's Health

Our Women’s Health Specialist GPs offer a wide range of professional services including contraception advice, sexual health, family planning and reproductive health, assistance with menstrual disturbances, Polycystic Ovarian Syndrome (PCOS) and menopause management.

Patient Information

Prescriptions

If you require a repeat prescription, please ensure you make an appointment with your doctor before you finish your medication.

Test Results

Please make an appointment with your doctor to discuss any questions you have about your test results.

Cancellation of Appointments

Please notify the clinic at least two hours before your appointment if you are unable to attend to ensure you avoid a late cancellation fee.

Messages

In certain circumstances your doctor may be able to speak to you over the phone. If this is the case your doctor will contact you once they have finished their consulting session, usually at the end of the day.

Encompass Medical Centre Fees

Payment is required at the time of consultation. Payment can be made by cash, EFTPOS, Visa or MasterCard.

Billing is at the discretion of each individual clinician. All fees are subject to change without notice.

This list is intended as a guide only and should be treated accordingly. For further fee information please speak to our reception staff or your doctor. Bulk billing is available for patients that meet certain criteria. Please ask our reception staff whether this applies to you.

Please present Pension or Centrelink Healthcare cards to reception staff when you arrive for your appointment.
